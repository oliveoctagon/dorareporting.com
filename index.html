Effortless reporting

We designed our DORA Reporting workflow to be as simple as possible, allowing businesses to comply with the new regulations without needing to invest time and money in complex or expensive solutions.

Simply fill out a spreadsheet in Excel with the details of your IT contractual arrangements, press a button, and instantly generate a fully DORA-compliant report which is ready to be uploaded to your national regulator.

Reliable validation

Our built-in data quality checks allow you to validate your final report before uploading it to your national regulator, giving you peace of mind that the file will be accepted, and reducing the likelihood of being asked to resubmit your data.

Validation checks can even be run on reports generated by third-party providers, meaning organisations that generate their report via a different system can still benefit from using our software.

Familiar design

For organisations familiar with the EBA's own conversion tool, which was provided for the 'Dry Run' exercise, our solution provides a great upgrade experience.

Our solution allows for a seamless transition from the EBA's conversion package to one built for your long-term reporting needs.

You can continue using familiar Excel spreadsheets, meaning you can get set up with our DORA Reporting workflow in minutes.
